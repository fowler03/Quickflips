Renal Failure - Acute

Both 1 and either 2 or 3 should be present.  Factors from 4 will lend supporting documentation.
  1. Patient not pursuing dialysis or renal transplant or is discontinuing dialysis
  2. Creatinine clearance <10 cc/min (<15 cc/min for diabetics) based on measurements or calculations; or <15cc/min (<20cc/min for diabetics) with comorbidity of CHF
  3. Serum creatinine >8.0 mg/dl (>6.0 mg/dl for diabetics)
  4. These comorbid conditions add supporting documentation:
  • Mechanical Ventilation
  • Malignancy in another organ system
  • Chronic lung disease
  • Advanced cardiac disease
  • Advanced liver disease
  • AIDS or immunosuppression
  • Albumin <3.5 gm/dl
  • Platelet count <25,000
  • Disseminated intravascular coagulation
  • GI bleeding
  • Sepsis
