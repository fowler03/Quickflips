Attending Physician

The patient may continue to see the attending physician for care related to the hospice diagnosis.  The attending physician bills Medicare Part B for those visits.

 

Any other doctor the patient sees for care related to the hospice diagnosis cannot bill Medicare Part B; if he/she does, the bills will be denied.  If the patient wants to see another doctor for care related to the hospice diagnosis, the hospice has to approve it and pay the doctor directly.
