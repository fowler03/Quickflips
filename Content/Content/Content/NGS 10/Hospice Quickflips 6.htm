Hospice Quickflips© #6

Cancer

 

  1. Disease with metastases at presentation

OR

  1. Progression from an earlier stage of disease to metastatic disease with either:
  • Continued decline in spite of therapy (like palliative chemo or radiation)

Or

  • Patient refuses further disease related therapy

 

Certain cancers with poor prognoses – small cell lung cancer, brain cancer and pancreatic cancer – may be hospice eligible without fulfilling the other criteria in this section.

 

Document a description of the decline and the impact of the decline on the patient’s functioning and quality of life.
