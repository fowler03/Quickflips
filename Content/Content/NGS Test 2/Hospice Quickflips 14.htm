Renal Failure - Chronic

Both 1 and either 2, 3, or 4 should be present.  Factor 5 will lend supporting documentation.

  1. Patient not pursuing dialysis or renal transplant, or is discontinuing dialysis
  2. Creatinine clearance <10 cc/min (<15 cc/min for diabetics); or <15cc/min (<20cc/min for diabetics) with comorbidity of CHF
  1. Serum creatinine >8.0 mg/dl (>6.0 mg/dl for diabetics)
  2. Signs and symptoms of renal failure, including:
  • Uremia
  • Oilguria (<400cc/24 hours)
  • Intractable hyperkalemia (>7.0) not responsive to treatment
  • Uremic pericarditis
  • Hepatorenal syndrome
  • Intractable fluid overload, not responsive to treatment
  1. It is also supportive to include the estimated GFR <10 ml/min
