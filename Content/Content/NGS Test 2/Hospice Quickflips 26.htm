Creating a “Culture of Eligibility”

Evaluating and documenting eligibility for hospice care requires that you incorporate the eligibility guidelines into your thinking, team process and documentation.   Here are some ideas for creating a “culture of eligibility.”

  • Be sure you have a current copy of the LCD guidelines.
  • Refer to the guidelines before each visit, and before you write your assessments and visit notes.
  • Make available a copy to the IDT, and review one with your team at the beginning of every meeting, (“LCD of the Week”).
  • Use LCD-specific worksheets when documenting admissions and recertifications.
  • Review the LCD guidelines for every admission and recertification before it is presented in IDT meeting.
  • In IDT meeting, rather than discuss patients by alphabet or by nurse, group them according to diagnosis, reviewing the LCD guideline before each group.
  • Hang poster-size copies of the guidelines in your IDT room.  Reference them.  Quiz each other on them.  Refer to them when reviewing patients.
