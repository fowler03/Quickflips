Introduction to Hospice Quickflips©

Part of our goal as hospice clinicians is to have our charts reflect:

  • Services that are reasonable and necessary for palliation and care of the terminal illness
  • Documentation that supports terminal illness with a life expectancy of six months or less, if the disease runs its normal course
  • Documentation that describes the patient’s condition accurately
  • Documentation that demonstrates the need for the level of care being provided
  • Understanding of the Medicare Conditions of Participation for hospice
  • Documentation that shows the quality of clinical intervention to effect patient outcomes
  • Documentation that allows for consistent and accurate data collection for quality assessment and improvement

 

Hospice Quickflips© are one-page guides to support excellent documentation.  They help you find the right words and focus.  Many are based on the Local Coverage Determinations provided by Medicare Administrative Contractor and the Medicare Conditions of Participation.  The hand-held format allows for gradual integration of a large amount of information into your professional practice and documentation.

 

Hospice Quickflips© can be used as:

  • A reference for documentation
  • A resource for discussions about eligibility
  • A teaching tool for hospice managers
  • An education tool for hospice marketers
  • A guide for referents

The Corridor Group is always interested in your comments, suggestions and questions about Hospice Quickflips©.
