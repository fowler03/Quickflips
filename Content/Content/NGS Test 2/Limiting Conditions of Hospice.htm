Limiting Conditions of Hospice Quickflips©

This guide is intended to encourage critical thinking and facilitate the discussion of the Medicare Conditions of Participation (COPs), eligibility, documentation, the interpretive guidelines, Medicare Administrative Contractor manuals, and efficiencies within hospice practice. 

It is not intended to serve as a substitute for the COPs or consultation with any regulatory agency.  It is not intended to be all-inclusive of regulatory requirements or standards of practice. 

In preparing this document, The Corridor Group (TCG) has provided key assumptions and information regarding local, regional and national trends, based on our judgment and information available at the time of publication.  TCG believes these assumptions are reasonable and appropriate.

Documentation guidelines may be interpreted differently under survey by state licensing or federal surveyors and Medicare Administrative Contractors.  The scope of this guide does not constitute a rendering of legal advice or legal opinions by TCG about clinical practice or regulations governing hospice care in your state or by federal oversight bodies.
