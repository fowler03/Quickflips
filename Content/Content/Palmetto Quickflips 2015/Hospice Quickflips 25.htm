Presenting a Case for Review

Suggested format:

  1. Name, age, gender, diagnosis and attending physician
  2. State each of the problems on the Plan of Care and then either:
  • Any changes in the past 2 weeks or
  • “Well managed with current interventions” or
  • “Resolved”
  1. Questions for the medical director or the team on any of these problems
  2. Were any new problems, goals or interventions added?  Any new orders needed?  Any change to visit frequencies?
  3. Repeat steps 2 through 4 for social work, spiritual care, volunteer and any other discipline involved
  4. Plan for next two weeks (until next IDT meeting or designated interval)
  5. Document the discussion and the plan (Plan of Care)

 

Think Differently:  How about starting with the social worker or spiritual care counselor, or have team members take turns facilitating the meeting?
