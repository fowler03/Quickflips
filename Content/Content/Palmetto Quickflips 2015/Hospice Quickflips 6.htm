ICF Worksheet - Cardiopulmonary

The medical review department will be utilizing an ICF worksheet to determine hospice eligibility for Cardiopulmonary Disease patients rather than look only at significant diagnosis codes.  The International Classification of Functioning, Disability and Health is published by the World Health Organization (WHO).  It provides for a standard language and framework for the description of health and health-related states.  It looks at body function, body structures, activities and participation and environmental factors.

 

For more information on ICF, go to www.who.int/classifications/icf/en/ and click on ICF online.
