Introduction to Therapy Quickflips ©

You are one of the lucky clinicians to be providing care in a truly holistic health care setting.  Home health therapists get to know patients and their families in their own homes, providing the opportunity for trust and rapport that can lead to better care and support.  Therapists align with patients in their territories, which can be one of the most effective ways to deliver health care.

Therapy Quickflips© is written with the therapist in mind.  In this guide, the therapist will find resources and guidance about regulations, best practice and documentation, all in a format that is easy to access in the field.

The focus of Therapy Quickflips© is on the documentation of care that reflects the professional skills performed by physical therapists, occupational therapists and speech-language pathologists, while demonstrating compliance and eligibility under the regulations and guidelines published by Medicare.  Other payors may have different requirements and it is important that you understand and follow those when caring for those patients.
