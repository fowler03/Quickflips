This guide is intended to encourage critical thinking and facilitate the discussion of the Medicare Conditions of Participation (COPs), eligibility, documentation, assessment, coding and efficiencies within home health care practice.

 

It is not intended to serve as a substitute for the COPs or consultation with any regulatory agency.  It is not intended to be all-inclusive of regulatory requirements, standards of practice or OASIS guidance.

 

In preparing this document, The Corridor Group (TCG) has provided key assumptions and information regarding local, regional and national trends, based on our judgment and information available at the time of publication.  TCG believes these assumptions are reasonable and appropriate.

 

Documentation guidelines may be interpreted differently under survey by state licensing or federal surveyors and the MACs.  The scope of this guide does not constitute a rendering of legal advice or legal opinions by TCG about clinical practice or regulations governing home health care in your state or by federal oversight bodies.
