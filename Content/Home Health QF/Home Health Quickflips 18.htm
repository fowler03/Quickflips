Home Health Quickflips© #18

Documenation Self-Check

 

Does my note:

  • Allow the reader to visualize the patient as I saw him or her?
  • Reflect the patient’s general inability to leave home without a taxing effort?
  • Demonstrate the need for the interventions of a skilled professional?
  • Contain objective data, such as:  vital signs, assessment of wound, measures, scales used in determining function/strength?
  • Reflect ongoing and timely communication and care coordination with the physician and other home health clinicians?
  • Reflect the skilled interventions and patient’s response/outcome for the particular visit?
  • Reflect the progress the patient is making toward his or her goals and planned discharge?
  • Reflect communication between home health clinicians, and with the patient and family?
  • Show the patient’s response to care/treatment provided?
  • Demonstrate the need to change the Plan of Care?

Is your note:

  • Signed and dated with full name and credentials?
  • Legible?
  • Timely?

Do you need to change the Plan of Care?

Do you need to communicate with the physician or other clinicians?

Is your documentation consistent with the Plan of Care, other notes and OASIS?
