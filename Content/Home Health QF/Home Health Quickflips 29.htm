M1200: Vision

 

This M item assesses the patient’s functional vision not visual acuity.  Assess the patient’s vision with any corrective lenses he or she usually wears.

  • Ask patient if he or she has had problems with vision, cataracts, glaucoma, etc.
  • Assess as patient signs forms.
  • Assess as you are identifying patient’s medications.
  • Reading glasses should be used if normally worn, but not a magnifying lens.
  • If patient is too cognitively impaired to assess, “non-responsive” may be indicated.

If the patient has severe visual limitations, there is a potential impact on other assessment items.  Be sure when appropriate, to clearly document this in other parts of the record, such as the Plan of Care under functional limitations, medication management, fall-safety risks and safety issues related to adaptive equipment.

 

Note: This item affects the clinical domain reimbursement.
