M1630: Ostomy for Bowel Elimination

 

t is helpful to break M1630 into a two-part question:

  • Does patient have an ostomy for bowel elimination?
  • Within the last 14 days has patient had an issue with the ostomy that caused either an inpatient stay or treatment changes?

Remember:

  • M1630 applies only to an ostomy for bowel elimination, for example, colostomy, ileostomy.
  • If an ostomy has been reversed and patient does not have an ostomy at time of assessment, then indicate “0.”
  • Even if an ostomy is not new, if it is related to the inpatient stay (for reasons such as diarrhea), indicate “2.”

 

Note: This M item affects both the clinical domain reimbursement and the non-routine supplies reimbursement.
