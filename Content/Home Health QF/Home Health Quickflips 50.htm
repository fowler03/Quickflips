M2030: Management of Injectables

 

This M item asks you to assess the patient’s current ability to take all injectable medications reliably and safely at all times. IV medications are excluded.

 

Included in this item:

  • All injections (IM, Subq) received in the home known, but not necessarily provided on day of assessment
  • One–time injections administered in the home (for example, flu shot)
  • Injections the physician orders the RN to administer in the home
  • PRN injectables are considered and reported for M2030 (for example, Epi-pen)

 

The patient must be viewed from a holistic perspective in assessing ability to perform medication management.  Look at:

  • Physical impairments
  • Emotional/cognitive/behavioral impairments
  • Sensory impairments
  • Environmental barriers

 

Interview the patient and/or caregiver, but also be sure to obtain orders for all medications and consult with the patient’s physician for the most accurate and complete information.

If the patient’s ability to administer one medication differs from his/her ability to administer another, enter the response based on the medication for which the patient requires the most assistance.

 

Note: This M item affects the clinical domain reimbursement.
