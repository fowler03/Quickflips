M2040: Prior Medication Management

 

M2040 Prior Medication Management: This item identifies the patient’s usual ability with managing oral and injectable medication prior to the onset of the current illness, exacerbation or injury that resulted in this episode of care.

  • If the patient’s prior ability varied between various oral or injectable drugs, select response based on the medication for which the most assistance was needed.  (Note: This is different from instructions on items such as Prior Functioning ADL/IADLs, in which the answer reflects ability to perform in a majority of the more frequently performed tasks.)
  • “Independent” means required no human assistance, including verbal assistance/reminders, to complete the task.
  • Reminders provided by a device that the patient can independently manage are not considered assistance or reminders.
  • “Needed some help” means that the patient required supervision or assistance from someone to take medications safely.
  • “Dependent” response should be selected if the patient was unable to perform any of the activity (for example, for oral medications, could only swallow the medications).
