M2200: Therapy Needed

 

This M item asks for a projection of the patient’s need for therapy, during the 60-day episode.  Medicare asks for the number of combined physical, occupational, and speech-language pathology visits projected for the upcoming 60-day episode of care.

 

Helpful hints:

  • M2200 is most accurately answered after a therapy assessment and Plan of Care (POC) is completed.  Be sure responses are relevant to the POC.
  • Collaborate with the physician and other clinicians during the 5 days allowed for the assessment. 
  • M2200 is primarily for Medicare patients and other payors who base payment on the HHRG score.  Indicate the number of visits needed.  Answer “000” if no therapy required.  “N/A” for patients that will not need the assessment to determine payment rate.
  • If start of care (SOC) or recertification/resumption of care (ROC), within last 5 days of current episode, this is your projection for the next 60 days.
  • Be sure your OASIS responses match the POC (CMS states that you can use your best estimate if the number of visits is uncertain).

 

What if your projection was wrong?

  • The Medicare billing system automatically adjusts the final payment to reflect the ACTUAL number of therapy visits performed.

 

Note: This item determines the Service Severity Domain for reimbursement.  It further impacts reimbursement by determining which “Equation” will be used to assign the payment group.
