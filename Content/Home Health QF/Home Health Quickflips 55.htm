M2401: Intervention Synopsis

 

This M item measures the use of “best practices” related to interventions for specific conditions and will be included in publicly reported quality measures.  It is completed at Discharge from Agency or Transfer.  To be marked as “yes,” the intervention must have been BOTH included in the physician-ordered plan of care AND implemented during or since the last OASIS assessment.

  • The physician-ordered plan of care means that the patient’s condition was discussed and there was agreement as to the plan of care between the clinician(s) and physician.
  • Interventions performed prior to the most recent OASIS assessment are not counted.
  • Interventions may have been completed by other clinicians and disciplines than the person completing the assessment.
  • For Diabetic Foot Care, BOTH monitoring AND education must have been ordered and performed in order to check “yes.”
  • For Pain Interventions, BOTH monitoring AND mitigation must have been performed in order to check “yes.”
  • To check "yes" on Depression Interventions, there must be orders for BOTH a best practice intervention(s) and evidence of implementation of the order(s)
  • “NA” can only be marked for falls prevention, depression interventions, pain intervention and pressure ulcer intervention if FORMAL assessments were performed and documentation revealed no risk on the relevant M1240, M1300, M1730 and M1910 at any time at or since the most recent SOC/ROC assessment.
