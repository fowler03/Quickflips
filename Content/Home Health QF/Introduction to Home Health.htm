Introduction to Home Health Quickflips©

 

You are one of the lucky clinicians to be providing care in a truly holistic health care setting.  Home health clinicians get to know patients and their families in their own homes, providing the opportunity for trust and rapport that can lead to better care and support.  We align with patients in their territories, which can be one of the most effective ways to deliver health care.

In this setting, the clinician functions independently and autonomously.  This is our strength, though some clinicians report feeling as if they were on an “island,” without the support of colleagues while in the home.  There are also many requirements for paperwork and documentation. 

Home Health Quickflips© is written with the clinician in mind.  In this guide, the clinician will find resources and guidance about regulations, best practice and documentation, all in a format that is easy to access in the field.

The focus of Home Health Quickflips© is on the documentation of care that reflects the professional skills performed by home health clinicians, while demonstrating compliance and eligibility under the regulations and guidelines published by Medicare.  Other payors may have different requirements and it is important that you understand and follow those when caring for those patients.
