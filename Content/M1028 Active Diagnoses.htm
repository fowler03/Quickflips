M1028: Active Diagnoses - Comorbidities and Co-Existing Conditions

 

This “M” item identifies whether two specific diagnoses are present, and active. These diagnoses influence a patient’s functional outcomes or increase a patient’s risk for development or worsening of pressure ulcer(s).

  1. Peripheral Vascular Disease (PVD) or Peripheral Arterial Disease (PAD)
  2. Diabetes Mellitus (DM)

 

To check either of these there must be a direct relationship to the patient’s current medical treatments, nurse monitoring, functional, cognitive, mood, or behavior status or risk of death at the time of the assessment.

 

Consider only diagnoses confirmed and documented by the physician. Reflect what is known at the time of the assessment.

 

A dash (-) value is a valid response for this item. A dash value indicates that no information is available and/or the item could not be assessed.

 

For more information, see Chapter 3 to OASIS-C2 Guidance Manual.
